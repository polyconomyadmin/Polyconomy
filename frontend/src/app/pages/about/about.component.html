About Us

Your guide to economic knowledge

Polyconomy is an AI assistant trained on economics research and literature. Developed in collaboration with the University of Buckingham and the Vinson Centre, it serves as a knowledgeable guide to economic concepts, theories, and ideas.

Whether you're a student diving into microeconomics, a researcher exploring macroeconomic theory, or a professional seeking to understand economic trends, Polyconomy provides clear, accessible explanations grounded in academic research.

Please note: Polyconomy provides information for understanding only and cannot offer personalised financial advice. Think of it as a guide to economic knowledge, not a decision-maker.
